Most companies are using AI to automate tasks.
The ones pulling ahead are using it to automate outcomes.

Here's the difference:

Automating a task:
→ Using AI to write a brief faster

→ Using AI to generate one social post

Automating an outcome:
→ Building a system that monitors market signals 
  and surfaces insights automatically

  optimises content without manual input
→ Designing AI agents that handle the entire 
  reporting cycle end to end

One is a productivity gain.
The other is a competitive advantage.

Most marketing teams I speak to are still in the 
first category. A few are building the second.

The gap between them is going to widen significantly 
over the next 12 months.

What are you building?
