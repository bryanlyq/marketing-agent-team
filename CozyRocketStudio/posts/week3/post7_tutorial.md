**Slide 3 — Step 1 (5 mins)**
```
Step 1 — Define your pillars
Open Claude / ChatGPT
Prompt:
"I create content about [NICHE].
My audience is [WHO].
Give me 3 content pillars
with 5 post ideas each."

Output: 15 ideas in 2 minutes
```

**Slide 4 — Step 2 (10 mins)**
```
Step 2 — Generate hooks
Prompt:
"Write 3 scroll-stopping hooks
for each post idea.
Format: curiosity / pain point / 
contrarian — one of each."

Output: 45 hooks to choose from
```

**Slide 5 — Step 3 (10 mins)**
```
Step 3 — Build your calendar
Open Notion (or Google Sheets)
→ Paste your posts in
→ Assign Week / Day / Platform / Format
→ Done. One month planned.
```

**Slide 6 — Step 4 (5 mins)**
```
Step 4 — Batch your captions

"Write Instagram captions for 
these 5 posts. Tone: [YOUR TONE].
Always end with a question."

Edit for your voice. Done.
```
