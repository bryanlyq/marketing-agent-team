## LINKEDIN TEXT POST

```
Why I'm building something outside my day job.

I want to be upfront: I'm not doing this because 
I'm unhappy at work. I'm doing it because I kept 
noticing a gap.

Every week, in meetings with marketing leaders across 
different industries, the same question surfaces:

"We know AI is important. We just don't know 
what to actually build."

Not lacking ambition. Not lacking budget.
Lacking a practical playbook.

So I started building one.

CozyRocketStudio documents everything I build at 
the intersection of AI, marketing, and growth.
The systems, tools, workflows, and results.

What building in public has taught me so far:

1. Practitioners have more credibility than theorists.
   People don't want AI advice from someone who read 
   about it. They want it from someone doing it.

2. The bar for "expert" is lower than you think.
   Most AI marketing content is either too basic or 
   too technical. The middle ground is wide open.

3. Building publicly makes you sharper.
   Having to explain what you build forces you 
   to understand it better.

If you're a marketer or business leader navigating AI 
— follow along. Everything I learn, I share.

#AI #MarketingLeadership #BuildInPublic 
#AIMarketing #Entrepreneurship #PersonalBrand
```
