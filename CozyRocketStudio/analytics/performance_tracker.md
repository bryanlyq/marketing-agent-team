## Key Metrics to Watch (2026 Algorithm Priorities)

**Instagram:**
- Saves > Likes (saves signal quality content)
- Completion rate on Reels (aim for >70%)
- Comments (algorithm boosts conversational posts)

**LinkedIn:**
- Comments > Reactions (deeper engagement = more reach)
- Dwell time (long-form posts keep people reading)
- Profile visits after a post (signals discovery)

**Substack:**
- Open rate (aim for >40% in Month 1)
- Reply rate (even 2-3 replies = high quality signal)
- Subscriber growth week on week
