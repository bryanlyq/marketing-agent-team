- Mix of consumer reviews, professional testing, and market research reports
- Sources include Garage Gym Reviews, Consumer Reports, BarBend (professional review sites) and market research firms (Mordor, IMARC, Fortune Business Insights)

### Data Limitations
- Some 2026 projections based on extrapolated trends
- Customer review scores vary by platform (Trustpilot vs. Amazon vs. brand site)
- Market share data most recent from 2022 (Statista) with 2026 estimates based on reports
- Under-desk treadmill market relatively new, smaller sample of reviews available
- Peloton FY2024 data (most recent available financial data)
