## "I Need to..." → Who to Ask

### Competitive Analysis
- **"Analyze competitor strategy"** → Research Intel + Social Media
- **"Monitor competitor ads"** → Social Media (ad libraries)
- **"What are competitors posting on social?"** → Social Media
- **"Track competitor product moves"** → Product Marketer + Research Intel

### Social Media
- **"Build our social content calendar"** → Content Creator (primary) + Social Media (insights)
- **"What's trending in our category?"** → Social Media
- **"Monitor our brand mentions/sentiment"** → Social Media
- **"Find influencer partnership opportunities"** → Social Media
- **"Analyze competitor social performance"** → Social Media

### Conversion & Growth
- **"Design A/B test for our homepage"** → Growth Agent (primary) + Content (copy) + Analytics (tracking)
- **"Improve conversion rate"** → Growth Agent (primary) + Research (insights) + Content (copy)
- **"Optimize landing page"** → Growth Agent (CRO) + Content (copy)

### Content
- **"Create blog post about [topic]"** → Content Creator
- **"Write email campaign"** → Content Creator
- **"Create social media posts"** → Content Creator (copy) + Social Media (strategy)
- **"Create ad copy variations"** → Content Creator + Paid Marketing

### Paid Advertising
- **"Launch ad campaign"** → Paid Marketing (primary) + Content (copy) + Social Media (competitive benchmarks)
- **"Optimize ad spend"** → Paid Marketing + Analytics
- **"Benchmark our ads vs competitors"** → Social Media (competitive analysis) + Paid Marketing (optimization)

### Measurement
- **"Track campaign performance"** → Analytics + campaign owner
- **"Set up dashboard"** → Analytics (setup) + campaign owner (metrics definition)
- **"Report on results"** → Analytics

### Product Launches
- **"Launch new product"** → Product Marketer (primary) + Research + Content + Paid + Analytics
- **"Position new feature"** → Product Marketer + Research

## Social Media Agent - Quick Lookup

### Core Capabilities

| Capability | What It Does | When to Use |
|---|---|---|
| **Social Monitoring** | Tracks competitor posts, engagement, content patterns | "What are competitors posting?" |
| **Ad Library Analysis** | Accesses Meta, Google, LinkedIn, TikTok ads | "What ads are competitors running?" |
| **Sentiment Analysis** | Tracks brand mentions and sentiment | "What are people saying about us?" |
| **Trend Detection** | Identifies trending topics and viral patterns | "What's trending in our category?" |
| **Influencer Analysis** | Evaluates influencer partnerships | "Who should we partner with?" |
| **Performance Comparison** | Benchmarks our metrics vs competitors | "How are we doing vs competitors?" |

### Typical Outputs

- **Ad Analysis Report** (competitor ad copy, creative, budget estimates)
- **Competitive Strategy Brief** (what competitors emphasize, how)
- **Trend Report** (emerging topics, viral patterns, content ideas)
- **Sentiment Dashboard** (brand mentions, sentiment tracking)
- **Influencer Assessment** (partnership recommendations)
- **Weekly Intelligence Memo** (all of above, condensed)

## Common Workflows at a Glance

### Workflow: Launch Optimization Campaign (3 weeks)

```
Week 1:
├─ Research Intel: Analyze competitor positioning
├─ Social Media: Monitor social sentiment & ads
└─ Growth Agent: Design test hypotheses

Week 2:
├─ Content Creator: Write copy variants
├─ Analytics: Set up tracking
└─ Growth Agent: Finalize test spec

Week 3+:
├─ Paid Marketing: Drive test traffic (optional)
├─ Analytics: Monitor & report
└─ Growth Agent: Analyze results & recommend winner
```

### Workflow: Content Strategy Development (2 weeks)

```
Week 1:
├─ Research Intel: Audience insights
├─ Social Media: Trend analysis & competitor content audit
└─ Content Creator: Proposes content pillars

Week 2:
├─ Social Media: Reviews calendar for trends
├─ Content Creator: Builds calendar
└─ Paid Marketing: Identifies amplification needs
```

### Workflow: Competitive Response (24 hours)

```
T+0 hours:
└─ Social Media detects & escalates

T+1 hour:
├─ Research Intel: Strategic assessment
├─ Product Marketer: Response options
└─ Content Creator: Creates response content
└─ Paid Marketing: Campaign structure

T+4 hours:
└─ Director: Decision & execution

T+24 hours:
└─ Analytics: Track performance vs. baseline
```

## Red Flags & Escalations

### Alert Director If:

🚨 **Immediate Action Needed**
- Competitor launches major campaign
- Negative sentiment spike detected
- Product security/crisis emerges
- Major influencer mentions product negatively

⚠️ **Strategic Decision Needed**
- A/B test shows opposite result
- Market shift detected
- Competitor positioning threatens ours
- Customer feedback contradicts strategy

📊 **Performance Issues**
- Campaign underperforming by >30%
- Engagement rates declining
- Conversion rate drops unexpectedly
