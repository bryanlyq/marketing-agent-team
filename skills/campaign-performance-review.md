# Campaign Performance Review

## Purpose
Conduct structured post-campaign reviews against pre-defined objectives, document what worked and what didn't, and feed learnings back into the next campaign planning cycle.

---

## When to Use This Skill
- At the close of any campaign with defined objectives
- Mid-campaign if results are significantly off-track
- Quarterly, to review all campaigns run in the period
- Before briefing a follow-on or repeat campaign

---

## Briefing Checklist
Before starting, confirm:
1. **Campaign objectives** — What were the stated goals and KPIs?
2. **Baseline** — What were the targets set before launch?
3. **Data access** — What campaign data is available and from which platforms?
4. **Audience** — Internal team, CMO, or client-facing?
5. **Scope** — Single campaign or portfolio review?

---

## Process

### Step 1 — Restate the Original Objectives
Never review performance without first anchoring to what success was supposed to look like:
- What were the primary and secondary KPIs?
- What were the targets for each?
- What was the budget and timeline?

If objectives weren't documented pre-launch, note this as a process gap.

### Step 2 — Compile Results Against Targets

| KPI | Target | Actual | Variance | Status |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Reach / Impressions | — | — | — | — |
| Clicks / CTR | — | — | — | — |
| Leads / MQLs | — | — | — | — |
| Pipeline generated | — | — | — | — |
| Conversion rate | — | — | — | — |
| Cost per outcome | — | — | — | — |
| Revenue influenced | — | — | — | — |

### Step 3 — Diagnose Performance Drivers
For each variance (positive or negative), identify the cause:

**Overperformance drivers:**
- Was it creative, audience, timing, channel, or offer?
- Is it repeatable or one-off?

**Underperformance drivers:**
- Was it a reach problem (not enough people saw it)?
- A relevance problem (wrong audience or message)?
- A conversion problem (landing page, offer, or friction)?
- An external factor (market, competitor, seasonality)?

### Step 4 — Extract Transferable Learnings
Translate findings into rules for future campaigns:

| What We Tested | What Happened | What to Do Next Time |
|---|---|---|
| — | — | — |

Structure learnings as:
- **Keep:** Tactics or approaches to repeat
- **Drop:** What to stop doing and why
- **Test:** Hypotheses to validate next time

### Step 5 — Update the Learning Repository
Log every campaign debrief in a shared learning repository so insights compound over time. Include:
- Campaign name and date
- Objective and result
- Top 3 learnings
- Owner who reviewed it

---

## Output Formats

### Campaign Debrief Report
- Campaign overview (objective, timeline, budget)
- Results vs targets (table)
- Performance diagnosis by channel/tactic
- Creative and audience analysis
- Top learnings (Keep/Drop/Test)
- Recommendations for next campaign

### Lessons Learned Document (condensed)
- What worked and why
- What didn't work and why
- 3 specific changes to make next time

### Portfolio Review (multi-campaign)
- Cross-campaign performance summary
- Patterns across campaigns (what consistently works/fails)
- Channel and creative efficiency rankings
- Strategic recommendations for next quarter

---

## Common Mistakes to Avoid
- **Reviewing without pre-set targets.** If there were no targets, there can be no fair review.
- **Attributing all results to one variable.** Campaigns are multi-variable; isolate causes carefully.
- **Writing up only when it went wrong.** Debrief every campaign — success contains as much learning as failure.
- **Vague learnings.** "Engagement was good" is not a learning. "Video content drove 3× CTR vs static on LinkedIn" is.
- **Not feeding learnings back.** A debrief that doesn't change anything is just documentation.

---

## Quality Check
Before submitting, ask:
- [ ] Are results compared against pre-set targets, not just prior period?
- [ ] Is every variance explained with a root cause?
- [ ] Are learnings specific and transferable to future campaigns?
- [ ] Have learnings been logged in the shared repository?
- [ ] Does the debrief end with specific changes to make next time?
